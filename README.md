Open-source calendar built for privacy. No ads. No trackers. No unnecessary permissions. Just a fast, reliable way to manage your schedule with a fully customizable interface — switch themes, adjust layouts, and make it yours. Day, week, month, year, and agenda views included. Your events stay on your device, always <br><br>
